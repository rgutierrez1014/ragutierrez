Project Description

If you have not figured out already, the 80s are my favorite decade. I also love many things inspired by the 80s. I love the aesthetic, the colors, the dramaticism. I love cyberpunk things and synthwave music.

I had Photoshopped an image of myself from the 2016 Pathways National Forum, one of Carnegie's two annual events. At the time, I was sleep-deprived and bored, and so I wrapped a bunch of Pathways lanyards around myself and had a colleague take a picture. I then messed with the coloring and added some lens flares in all the right places.

For a while, that's all it was, a Photoshopped image of myself. I played around with some 80's-inspired fonts. In July of this year, it was my colleague's birthday, and I decided to use the image and the 80's fonts to make a custom birthday card.
